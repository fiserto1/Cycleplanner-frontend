About

Bicycle Route Planner is capable of computing routes for cyclists from an origin to a destination. It solves the bicycle route planning problem based on a formal model expressive enough to represent transport network features and cyclists' preferences grounded in the studies of real-world bicycle route choice behaviour. Our solution employs the A* algorithm together with vectors of cost and heuristic functions – able to optimise routes for travel time, comfort, quietness, and flatness.
